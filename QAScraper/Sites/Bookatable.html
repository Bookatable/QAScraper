More about Prezzo - Thame

Full of character and history, this Prezzo is housed in a beautiful Tudor-style building. In the centre of Thame, Prezzo provides the perfect setting to enjoy modern Italian cuisine.

Featuring award-winning pizzas, the Prezzo menu also features a great range of risottos, pasta and salads, and there are delicious specials that change regularly. Prezzo restaurant offers diners a good choice of tasty antipasti dishes, in addition to speciality folded calzone pizzas. The indulgent dessert list reveals Italian favourites like tiramisu and panna cotta, while the extensive wine list has a good range of Italian bottles.

With attentive and friendly service, Prezzo – Thame offers stylish Italian dining at affordable prices. There is a popular kids menu, along with a good range of vegetarian options.

Close to the High Street in Thame, Prezzo is in a picturesque setting and has a warm, lively ambience. A popular Italian restaurant, this Prezzo is a local favourite for contemporary dining.
