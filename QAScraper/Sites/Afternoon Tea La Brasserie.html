La Brasserie has been a landmark London eatery since 1972, bringing Parisian chic to Chelsea. Trends may come and go, but La Brasserie retains its place in the market by gradual adjustment to the modern world without losing its old world charm. 

Oozing effortless elegance, La Brasserie offers a ritzy bar and the black and white tiling indoors are offset by laidback pavement dining to create an atmosphere perfect for Afternoon Tea. 

South Kensington Tube Station is just 4 minutes walk away and Harrods only a 10 minute walk.
